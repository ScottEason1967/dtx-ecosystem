Position papers

The formal positions HomeTest Commercial has adopted on the interim commercial machinery. Each paper crystallises a single decision, the rationale behind it, the triggers that would reopen it, and the dependencies it rests on. Each opens in a click and prints clean as a one-pager.

What these papers are. Position papers are short, declarative statements of the positions HomeTest Commercial has formally adopted. They are derived from the interim commercial playbook but designed to be read standalone, quoted in meetings, attached to emails and circulated without requiring the reader to navigate the playbook to find them. Each paper links back to the relevant plays for the operational detail.

Framework status: operating in interim shape

No formal commercial framework in the FY26–FY27 window. Operating via the Assurance wrapper with bilateral contracting. Named reopen triggers govern when the framework conversation reopens.
