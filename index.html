NHS Digital Therapeutics ecosystem

A single way in to the work across the DTx programmes. The strategic map, the operating model architecture, the operating playbooks, the formal positions adopted and the engagement materials that bring it to life. Three products in scope: HomeTest, HealthStore, HealthSync. Pick a door.

Who this is for. NHSE colleagues working on or alongside the DTx commercial programmes. HomeTest is the live product today; HealthStore and HealthSync follow. Programme leads, commercial teams, IG and clinical leads, sponsors, NHS Legal contacts, finance, integration partners. Each door takes you to a specific part of the landscape. Doors marked In scoping are work committed but not yet built.

How the artefacts fit together

The five spine artefacts form a stack. The Map sits at the top as the strategic front door. The DTx OM sits behind it as the architectural reference. The Playbooks sit in the middle as the operations layer. The Position Papers sit below as the formal stated positions. The Hearts and Minds materials sit alongside as the persuasion layer drawn from the OM's character work.
