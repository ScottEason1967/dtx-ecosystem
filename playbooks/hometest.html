HomeTest playbooks

Operating manuals for the teams running HomeTest day-to-day. Commercial is live. Clinical, IG, Platform, Engagement and Strategic Finance are on the roadmap.

What lives here. Each HomeTest playbook describes how a specific function runs day-to-day. The Commercial Playbook is the live operating manual following the interim shape. Other functions (Clinical, IG, Platform, Engagement, Strategic Finance) currently sit inside the Commercial Playbook's cross-cutting plays or the DTx Operating Model; standalone playbooks for each will be built as the operating model matures.
