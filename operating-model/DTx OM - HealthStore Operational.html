HealthStore Operational Swimlanes (HealthStore overlay)

How HealthStore runs at steady state. This page is the HealthStore operational overlay within the Digital Therapeutics Operating Model. A portfolio Operational hub page (with HomeTest and HealthSync overlays alongside) will sit above this. Seven operational processes, seven actors. The patient is one of them: Pauline as the lead patient archetype, with further therapeutic areas adding their own patient archetypes as services come on. The other six are the people who prescribe, supply, govern and operate the service around the patient.

How to read this

Eight operational lanes, each describing a distinct process at steady state. Foundation-marked cells carry across all Digital Therapeutics products; Overlay-marked cells are HealthStore-specific. Where a cell is empty, that actor has no role at that stage. How to read each lane: columns flow left to right in time (Find → Decide → Onboard → Go live → Operate); cells stacked within a single column are concurrent — each actor doing their part at that stage, not happening in sequence.

This is the steady-state operating shape, not the route to get there. Today HealthStore is in design with the lead configuration progressing through the conditional NICE EVA route and a pipeline of admitted suppliers behind it. Discovery work and supplier admission cycles sit outside scope. Day-1 launch mechanics will be interim, with this page describing the steady state once a service reaches Commissioner Store v1.

Six foundation-level capabilities sit alongside these lanes: Post-market surveillance (regulatory: MHRA, adverse events, periodic safety updates), Pathway integration (alignment with national transformation programmes per therapeutic area), Independent assurance (third-party assurance distinct from internal Clinical Safety), Contract Management (post-execution lifecycle for each call-off), Ongoing Evidence Generation (continuous evidence accrual into the Assurance Model), and the Service Wrapper anatomy (six-touchpoint patient chain plus four local-pathway items). All cross-product capabilities, surfaced in the Teamwork view (Cap-12 to Cap-16), the Anatomy Built foundation and the Foundation RACI.

What the swimlanes decompose to
Service blueprint frame

Four service roles. Eight operational lanes.

The HealthStore service breaks into four high-level roles that any admitted digital therapeutic has to be served by. The eight operational lanes above decompose those four roles into the actor-by-actor process detail. Read the four roles first if you want the shape; drop into the lanes for the mechanics.

1. Authorisation for NHS use
National decision that a digital therapeutic is clinically and economically suitable for the HealthStore. Maps to lanes 2 (Supplier onboarding) and 6 (Performance & assurance).
2. NHS App onboarding
Technical integration of the admitted digital therapeutic into the NHS App so a prescription becomes a patient-usable experience. Maps to lanes 2 (Supplier onboarding) and 8 (Market dynamics).
3. NHS App experience
How the digital therapeutic actually works for the patient: find, sign-in, launch, manage, escalate, return. Maps to lanes 3 (Prescribing workflow) and 4 (Service delivery).
4. Outcome capture & ongoing assurance
How the digital therapeutic produces and proves value over time: outcome capture, milestone settlement, clinical safety monitoring, evidence accrual, IG oversight. Maps to lanes 5 (Financial settlement), 6 (Performance & assurance) and 7 (Information governance).
