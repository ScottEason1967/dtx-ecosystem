About this role

Suppliers are the institutions that deliver the capability the platform routes to. The role threads through every product, but the institution and the operating model behind it vary per product.

HomeTest needs a supplier (or supplier consortium) that can dispatch kits to patients' homes, analyse samples in an accredited lab, and return results through the platform. Joe is the archetype. The supplier model varies by configuration: all-inclusive black-box (kit plus lab plus result through one supplier); split (logistics supplier plus specialist lab as separate suppliers); pathology-led (NHS pathology lab anchors the chain, others wrap around). The black-box shape is the working assumption for HIV; PSA-AS and iron deficiency anaemia shapes will firm up as those configurations approach commissioning.

HealthStore needs a supplier that holds the digital therapeutic product itself: the founder or sponsor whose product is admitted to the National App Formulary and prescribed through the HealthStore route. Marcus is the archetype. The operating relationship runs through formulary admission, evidence requirements and pricing architecture rather than a per-kit dispatch model. Different commercial shape; same role.

HealthSync needs a supplier that brings device-side data into NHS care pathways: the integration partner who supplies the wearable hardware, the data stream and the integration layer. Tariq is the archetype. Earliest stage of the three; operating shape will firm up as configurations land.

This role is distinct from David (NHS Commercial), who sits in the universal hub as the central commercial framework holder. David shapes the framework, vehicle and pricing architecture that suppliers compete inside. Joe, Marcus and Tariq sit on the receiving side of that framework, configuring their offers to the NHSE rules of engagement.

Why one role, three personas The four central platform roles (Aisha clinical / CSG, Priya NHSE Platform, David NHS Commercial, Ngozi IG) are universal in person: one person each, identical across every product. The Supplier role is different. The institution that supplies a given product varies (test supplier for HomeTest, DTx founder for HealthStore, integration partner for HealthSync), so the cast is one named persona per product. Joe is the HomeTest supplier archetype; Marcus is the HealthStore supplier archetype; Tariq is the HealthSync supplier archetype.
