Product · HomeTest

HomeTest.

A home-based diagnostic testing service. A clinical-grade test kit is dispatched to the patient's door, the sample is returned to a lab, and the result lands back in the patient's NHS App and clinical record. Three patient initiation patterns serve different population groups today: patient-initiated (NHS App-driven self-screening, Abdul, CVKM), clinician-initiated (acute consultant request, Mark, PSA active surveillance) and GP-initiated (primary-care arranged monitoring, Layla, HbA1c).

1HomeTest in the four-level anatomy
Level 1 · Foundation
Digital Therapeutics platform layer
HomeTest runs on the Digital Therapeutics platform layer: NHS Login for patient identity, NHS Notify for messaging, the NHS App for patient surface, FHIR routing for clinical data, the contracting instrument that admits suppliers to the approved list (interim shape: PSR plus standard NHS call-off plus approved list; PA23 dynamic market the leading candidate if the framework conversation reopens), the IG framework structure, the hazard log structure, the assurance gateway and the SHAPE / ASSURE / CONTRACT / FULFIL / MEASURE capability loop. The platform layer is the same across every Digital Therapeutics product; HomeTest consumes it without owning or building it.
Level 2 · HomeTest overlay
Kit, lab, postal logistics, results back to the patient
The HomeTest overlay covers the eight dimensions where this product expresses itself distinctively: three initiation patterns (patient, clinician, GP), commissioning routes split by initiation (LA for HIV, acute trust for PSA-AS, GP for HbA1c), all-inclusive supplier or specialist lab + logistics depending on the configuration, per-completed-test commercial model, free-at-the-point-of-care tariff, UKAS-accredited lab pathway, condition-specific hazard register, NHS Standard Contract under existing framework instruments (SBS Sexual Health Lot 1 for HIV). This is the layer where HomeTest looks and behaves like HomeTest.
Level 3 · Pattern picks
The reusable options HomeTest selects
Three initiation patterns from the catalogue (patient-init, clinician-init, GP-init). Two supply-chain patterns (all-inclusive supplier; specialist lab plus logistics). UKAS-accredited lab requirement. Per-completed-test commercial pattern. LA / Acute / GP commissioner patterns. Different patterns combine per configuration; each combination is one configuration.
Level 4 · Configurations
Live and in-design deployments
HIV live Beta in two LA-supplier pairings under the framework. PSA-AS in design (acute trust consultant requesting capillary draw at home, specialist lab pathway, NHS pathology network alternatives being modelled). HbA1c in design (GP-arranged routine monitoring, result returns to the GP record). Each configuration sits inside the HomeTest overlay; further configurations come online as therapeutic areas come into scope.
2Live and in-design configurations
HIV
Live Beta · patient-initiated
LA-commissioned in two areas. Patient orders via the NHS App. Supplier fulfils end-to-end: kit dispatch, sample analysis, result return to the App. Per-completed-test commercial. NHS Standard Contract via a sexual-health framework call-off. Two live supplier pairings under the framework.
PSA-AS
In design · clinician-initiated
Consultant requests as part of the active surveillance pathway for low-grade prostate cancer. Capillary draw at home. Specialist lab analyses; logistics split between the kit supplier and the lab. Clinician sees the result first, mediates with the patient. Acute trust governance, activity-based commercial. Not yet deployed.
HbA1c
In design · GP-initiated
GP arranges the kit as part of diabetes monitoring (Pathway 3). Kit to home. Result lands in the GP record; only contacted if action needed. Focused on relieving GP pressure for routine review. Pattern picks are working assumptions; several entries TBC.
3HomeTest through the lifecycle
Shape
Demand generation. Therapeutic area selection (HIV, PSA, HbA1c). Pathway and equity assessment.
Assure
DCB0129 / 0160, UKAS lab accreditation, hazard log, Clinical Safety Case Report. EScAL, DTAC, SLSP, ROPA, DPIA.
Contract
NHS Standard Contract through framework call-off (SBS Lot 1 for HIV). Acute trust contracting for PSA-AS. Primary-care commissioning for HbA1c.
Fulfil
Patient or clinician orders. Kit dispatched. Sample returned. Lab analyses. Result returned to App or clinical record.
Measure
Activity, equity, pathway-level outcome, value comparison against in-clinic testing. Feedback into the next round of shaping.
