About this role

Clinicians are the people who initiate, prescribe, interpret or sign off on what the platform delivers. The role threads through every product, but the specific clinician changes depending on what the platform is doing for the patient.

HomeTest clinician-initiated configurations need a treating clinician who sets the intent to order and interprets the result (Adam, consultant urologist, PSA Active Surveillance). HomeTest GP-initiated configurations need a GP archetype who initiates routine orders during long-term-condition reviews (Hannah, GP, iron deficiency anaemia). HealthStore needs a prescribing clinician who admits the patient to the digital therapeutic (Ravi). HealthSync will need a clinician on the care team monitoring the data once the first configuration lands.

This role is distinct from Aisha, who sits in the universal hub as the central clinical safety / CSG role. Aisha is platform-level clinical governance; Adam and Ravi are treating clinicians at the point of care.

Why one role, multiple personas The four central platform roles (Aisha clinical / CSG, Priya NHSE Platform, David NHS Commercial, Ngozi IG) are universal in person: one person each, identical across every product. The Treating Clinician role is different. Clinicians are at the point of care, and the point of care varies by product. Adam is the HomeTest clinician-initiated archetype; Ravi is the HealthStore prescribing-clinician archetype. Future personas will sit alongside as new configurations land.
