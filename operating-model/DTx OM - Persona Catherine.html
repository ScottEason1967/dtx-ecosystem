Catherine.

Acute Trust commissioner · PSA Active Surveillance · established radiotherapy-follow-up cohort
“The clinical case is settled. I need the operational shape, the tariff envelope and the contract route to hold up at audit. Once that lands, my Trust can adopt.”
Catherine is the commissioner at an acute trust who holds the budget for PSA Active Surveillance services in the radiotherapy-follow-up cohort. She commissions secondary-care services under the tariff envelope rather than a separate budget line. Reports through the Trust's Director of Operations and the Director of Finance. She is Mark's commissioner.

Portrait commissioning

What she commissions

The PSA Active Surveillance service for men post-radiotherapy who are now in post-treatment PSA surveillance, monitoring for biochemical recurrence. The established cohort: clinically defined, clinically owned by Urology, sitting inside the acute trust's pathway. Mark is the patient archetype on this cohort.

What she needs from HomeTest

Three things, in order.

  • A service the consultant urologist will adopt. The clinical adoption test sits with Adam (or the equivalent treating clinician in her trust). Without clinical buy-in, no order is initiated and no test happens.
  • A tariff envelope that holds. The PSA Active Surveillance Monitoring Episode tariff is in design with NHSE Strategic Finance; until that lands, Catherine is commissioning against a tariff position that is still being worked through.
  • A contract route her procurement team will sign off. Standard call-off under a framework her Trust can lean on. She does not have the headcount to run a bespoke procurement per pathway.

Where Catherine sits relative to the other commissioners

Catherine is one of several commissioners across the portfolio. Sarah commissions HomeTest HIV from the Local Authority sexual health budget. Ruth will commission the PSA-AS monitoring-gap cohort once that cohort's commissioning shape lands. Anita will commission HbA1c from the ICB or GP side. Daniel will commission HealthStore COPD Alpha at ICB level. The same role (commissioner) lives behind each, but the institution and budget are configuration-specific. Catherine is the established acute-side commissioner anchor.
