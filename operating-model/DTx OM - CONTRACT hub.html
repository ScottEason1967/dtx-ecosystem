The shared mechanic across products

What holds at CONTRACT across the portfolio

Every product runs through CONTRACT. The shared mechanic is the contracting instrument (per product), the supplier onboarding gateway and the commissioner buying journey (Find / Decide / Onboard / Go live / Operate). The product-specific variation lives in the contract instrument, the commercial model and the pricing mechanism. For HomeTest, the contracting instrument in the current window is the Step 1 interim route (six-month SBS PSR bridge for HIV; Alpha/Beta MOUs continuing for live deployments), with the Step 2 steady-state vehicle decision (PA23 vs PSR, dual-route Plan A / B / C) under SLT alignment. For HealthStore it is ACE plus the NHS Standard Contract.
