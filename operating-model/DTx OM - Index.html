Digital Therapeutics · Operating model

One operating model. Three products. Built around real people.

A portfolio-level operating model covering HomeTest (testing at home, with a clear result), HealthStore (prescribed digital therapeutics delivered through an app) and HealthSync. Designed to extend to future Digital Therapeutics programmes without rework. One architecture across all three. The same operating roles, the same lifecycle, the same assurance discipline.

Every operating model has to answer five questions. WHY it exists, at the centre. WHAT it is composed of, WHEN things happen, HOW people work day to day, and WHO is accountable around it.

The cast

Universal roles at the centre. Product-specific actors around them.

An operating model is a set of relationships between people. NHSE orchestrates the market; the people do the work. Six universal operating roles thread through every Digital Therapeutics product. Four are platform-level (Aisha clinical / CSG, Priya NHSE Platform, David NHS Commercial, Ngozi IG): one person each, identical across products. Two are role archetypes (Commissioner, Clinician): the role threads through every product, but the institution or specialty behind it varies per configuration. Product-specific actors sit around the wraparound: HomeTest, HealthStore and HealthSync each have their own supplier archetype and patient archetypes.
