The Map

The Map to 2030

Year 1 today, proving the foundations. Year 4 in 2030, when DTx is part of routine NHS care. Four things move together to get there: the tests landing and ramping, the vehicle we run them on, the assurance bar that makes them safe, and the audiences we have to bring with us.
What changed from v1
  • Timeline shifted left a year. Starts at FY 26/27 (current proving year), ends at FY 29/30 (the 2030 endpoint). "Beyond" rail captures FY 30/31 onwards.
  • Year themes added to header from programme planning language (Proving / Ready to scale / Scaling / Routine care).
  • Hearts & Minds character placements moved to match real target dates. Marcus and Sarah now anchor in FY 26/27 (supplier onboarding + Commissioner Store prototype + 2 ICB partners are all this year). Ravi at FY 26/27 → 27/28 transition (COPD beta to MVP). Errol holds at FY 27/28 (cross-product needs HIV public + COPD MVP both live).
  • Terrence Higgins Trust February 2027 proposition added to Q4 FY 26/27 — major HIV beta milestone I missed in v1.
  • Scale lane carries confirmed per-FY test volumes (5/10/15/20% utilisation ramp) plus the current-year HIV and COPD proven volumes (10k kits, 500 new COPD patients).
  • Decisions rail uses quarter tags (Q1-Q4) where the source has them.
  • Real character portraits where placed (Marcus, Ravi, Sarah, Errol).

Visual changes from v1

Single white canvas with subtle lane background tints rather than every cell as a separate card. Cleaner timeline header with NHS dark blue and a year-theme line under each FY. Decisions rail uses Q1-Q4 quarter tags where the primary source has them. Hearts & Minds now uses real portraits in proper circular frames anchored at the right point in time. Volume bands at the bottom of each Scale column show ramped totals at glance. Vehicle lane reads as one continuous band (Now → PSR → Gate → PA23 → Beyond) rather than separate blocks.

Still not happy with: (a) Scale lane is text-heavy in the middle years; want a thin SVG volume curve drawn behind the chips showing the ramp shape, (b) the Vehicle decision gate could be a stronger vertical break across all lanes (a hard line at the end of FY 28/29), (c) overall the canvas is dense — could benefit from being scroll-horizontal at narrower viewports rather than compressing.

Judgment calls still open

Where does the framework decision gate sit? Currently at end of FY 28/29 / start of FY 29/30 — i.e. two and a bit years of PSR matchmaker before the call. Aligns with Matt's "do less, prove, prove" but might be too generous a runway. Could be a year earlier (end FY 27/28).
HealthStore and HealthSync on the Scale lane. Currently only COPD (HS) and HIV (HT) are shown as "proven live" in FY 26/27. The Scale lane from FY 27/28 onwards is dominated by the 10 HomeTest tests because that's what the spreadsheet covers. Should we (a) keep it HomeTest-tests-focused and accept HealthStore/HealthSync appear only in Y1, (b) add TBC-placeholder HealthStore use cases as later waves (UC2 Cardiac Rehab, UC3 MSK), (c) build a parallel Map per workstream?
"Sceptical ICBs" and "NHSE Commercial counterparts" framing. These are real audiences from Matt's analysis but the language could read as sniping. Soften to "later-wave ICBs" and "NHSE Commercial colleagues"? Or keep blunt because that's the political reality?
The "Now" vehicle block (FY 26/27). I've drawn it as "Direct supplier contracts via ACE / existing routes — not built to scale". This is true but it's possibly more honest than the current artefact says publicly. Comfortable having that on the front door?
