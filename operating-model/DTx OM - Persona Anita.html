Anita.

ICB / GP commissioner · HomeTest HbA1c
“Primary care has nowhere to put this on the contract today. Until the QOF route or the outpatient tariff is settled, I do not have a clean place to commission from. Tell me where the money sits and I will buy.”
Anita is the commissioner at an Integrated Care Board who holds the budget for primary-care diabetes monitoring. The commissioning route for HbA1c at-home testing sits between the ICB pathway envelope and the GP practice contract. She is Layla's commissioner on the HomeTest HbA1c configuration.
Portrait commissioning

What she commissions

HomeTest HbA1c for patients on the routine diabetes monitoring cycle in primary care. The configuration is in design phase. Anita is the commissioner who will hold the budget once the funding route is settled.

What she needs from HomeTest

  • A funding route that fits primary care. QOF (Quality and Outcomes Framework) is one option but does not sit inside the current 2027/28 tariff window for HbA1c specifically. Outpatient tariff is another option. The primary-care contract mechanism is a third. Until the route is settled, the buying decision is stuck.
  • GP-side adoption. The GP arranges the test in this configuration; the result returns to the GP record. Without GP buy-in, no order is initiated.
  • Integration with the existing diabetes review cycle. The patient experience anchors on the existing primary-care review rhythm, not a stand-alone result delivery. The HbA1c result has to land in the GP system at the right point in the patient's annual review.

Where Anita sits relative to the other commissioners

Anita is one of several commissioners in the portfolio. Sarah commissions HomeTest HIV from the LA sexual health budget. Catherine commissions PSA-AS established cohort from the acute trust. Ruth will commission the PSA-AS monitoring-gap cohort. Daniel commissions HealthStore COPD Alpha at ICB level. Same role; different institution per configuration.

Where Anita appears in the model

  • ICB / GP Commissioner role in the HbA1c configuration RACI on the Who page once HbA1c governance lands
  • Commissioning overlay on the Pattern Overlay Detail page (HbA1c row)
  • Anita is the commissioner anchored to Layla's cohort
Status. Anita is a design-phase persona. The clinical case for HbA1c at-home testing is strong; the commissioning route through ICB or GP-practice contract is the open question. The funding route question may resolve in the FY28/29 tariff cycle.
