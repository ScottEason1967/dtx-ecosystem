Matchmaker with assurance
Approved list. Published assurance standard. Standard call-off contract between supplier and commissioner. NHSE convenes, assures, introduces. Not a party to the contract. No percentage take. The live operating model.

Matchmaker maturing
Wave 1 + 2 live. Evidence accruing. Reopen triggers tracked: volume threshold, supplier demand, commercial case.
Reopen gate
Framework yes/no?
Have reopen triggers fired? If yes, what kind of framework? PA23 leads as candidate. Matchmaker may continue indefinitely if triggers don't justify the standup cost.
2030+
Steady state
Either matchmaker at maturity, or formal framework (PA23 leading) with commercial layer activated. Decision dependent on what proves out.
