About this stage

Platform-agnostic function. SHAPE operates across all products. It is where the market is understood, where demand is generated, and where new products may be conceived based on feedback from MEASURE.

Key activities: identifying which conditions or pathways to prioritise next, building commissioner confidence and capability, running engagement cycles, and translating performance data into strategic direction.

This is the entry point for the iterative loop. Without SHAPE receiving and acting on MEASURE outputs, the model is linear and static. With it, the platform continuously adapts.
